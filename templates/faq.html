Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vesta?

Vesta is a financial tracking tool that provides data such as a companies daily support & resistance data.

What is a support?

Supports are the price level that an asset does not fall below for period of time. The simple support level can be charted by drawing a line along the lowest lows for the time period being considered.

What is a resistance?

Resistances are the price point at which the rise in the price of an asset is halted by the emergence of a growing number of sellers who wish to sell the at that price. The simple resistance level can be charted by drawing a line along the highest highs for the time period being considered.

Are there any future plans for Vesta?

Vesta is always improving! The most recent future state will notify users when support & resistance levels are met.
